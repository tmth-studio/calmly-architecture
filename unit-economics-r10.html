Final model scope: This unit economics model reflects all 10 design requirements (R4–R10) and the C3 architecture. Three improvements are not modelled — they are upside, not baseline: (1) CLV for repeat-use segments (tenants, small businesses) raises per-consumer revenue without raising per-claim cost; (2) multi-org assessment fee competition may reduce £16 assessment fee to £10–12 at scale; (3) data flywheel improves pool operator pricing as EV accuracy compounds. Fin-sim rebuild (C3 condition) must incorporate these before Phase III capital. Gate: PASS — 66% FMOS, full F2 architecture confirmed.
