Section 1 — Strategic design: R4 Want Block

Implicit Theory of Change (claimant)

Consumer's implicit ToC: "Success in recovering my money depends on professional legal representation that I cannot afford relative to the claim value." The system appears designed for businesses. The market structure itself — no affordable options exist — signals the problem is unsolvable cheaply.

Key efficacy doubt: Credibility gap — "If affordable small claims resolution were possible, established legal firms would already offer it at scale." The absence of visible alternatives is taken as evidence of impossibility.

Secondary: Inertia — the claim is weeks old, acute frustration has faded, adaptation has set in.

Theory of Change — Attraction (CMO format)

Class of problem: Credibility asymmetry + market structure signalling.

Theory: Spence (1973) — independent signal resolves quality uncertainty. The independent assessment org (designed in C3 for defendant/investor credibility) simultaneously resolves claimant credibility doubt. Same mechanism, two audiences.

Context: Consumers who have received a company's final refusal and are searching for options. Peak receptivity — injustice fresh, not yet adapted.

Mechanism: A free independent EV assessment of the consumer's specific claim from a named university law department (48-hour turnaround) challenges the prior belief that resolution cannot be affordable. The assessment demonstrates the model by doing real work before any commitment is required.

Outcome: Consumer has independent, credible assessment of their specific claim's value and a specific Calmly offer, and forms sufficient conviction to decide whether to proceed.

Attraction Strategy

Free independent EV assessment as conviction mechanism. Consumer submits dispute details → assessment org produces free EV report within 48hrs → Calmly makes specific offer. The same institutional actor that makes the model credible to investors makes it credible to claimants. One mechanism, two audiences. Reach: organic SEO + word of mouth at the category entry point (consumers searching for help after receiving a final refusal).

Cross-requirement elegance

The C3 independent assessment org is the R4 attraction mechanism. No additional institutional actor needed. The free EV assessment also pre-frames the R6 amortisation strategy — it establishes expected value as the reference point before Calmly's offer arrives. C3 architecture generates three downstream benefits: investor credibility (C3), claimant conviction (R4), offer framing (R6).

Cumulative BFF (C1–R4)

Calmly is a claims origination and litigation risk market infrastructure business. When consumers experience a financial dispute and receive a final refusal, organic discovery (SEO at point of peak receptivity) brings them to Calmly. The independent assessment org provides a free EV assessment within 48 hours — simultaneously resolving claimant credibility doubt and generating investor-grade portfolio data. Consumers who accept transfer their claim to the platform. Pool operators acquire pools of assessed claims; LMU specialists execute the pre-trial bundle strategy. Claims settle early (majority) or proceed to court under pool operator risk. Calmly earns platform fees per resolved claim.

Section 3 — I: Intrinsic performance capability
Pass

CAC has an intrinsic dilution mechanism. Organic SEO amortisation is a fixed content investment — as claims volume grows, the per-claim CAC falls without additional spend. Word of mouth is zero marginal cost and grows with satisfied claimants. The architecture's free EV assessment also functions as a product-led growth loop (consumers share their outcome, driving organic referral). At 1,500 claims/month, word of mouth is expected to account for 40–50% of inflow — further reducing effective CAC below £38. This is a structural property, not an operational target. Pass.

Section 4 — T: Theorem-driven design
Pass

Attraction — Spence (1973) quality signalling

The free independent EV assessment resolves credibility doubt by providing direct personal evidence — the assessment org does real work before commitment. This is Spence (1973) applied to the consumer adoption context: an independent, costly-to-fake signal eliminates the consumer's quality uncertainty. Well-replicated theory; the specific application to consumer services (not just labour markets) has extensive precedent in the credence goods literature. Pass.

Reach — category entry point SEO

Consumers searching after receiving a company's final refusal are at peak receptivity — the job-to-be-done is salient, urgency is high, and search intent is transactional. Organic SEO at this specific query set (e.g. "company won't refund what to do", "small claims court worth it") is a documented content marketing strategy in consumer fintech and legal tech (MoneySavingExpert, Resolver, Which? all follow this model). No novel theory required — empirically grounded in established category practice. Pass.

Section 6 — Conditions before capital commitment
  1. Condition 1Validate organic CAC assumption (£38/claim). Tier 4 — analogous to consumer fintech organic-first models. Validate through pilot data: track cost per content piece, monthly organic search traffic, claim conversion rate, and word of mouth share. At pilot scale (40 claims/month), CAC will be much higher. The £38 is an at-scale projection.
  2. Condition 2Confirm category entry point queries. Validate the specific search queries at which consumers are most receptive (post-final-refusal). Competitor content audit (Resolver, Which?, Citizens Advice) should inform SEO keyword strategy before content investment begins.
  3. Condition 3Measure free EV assessment conversion rate. The core R4 assumption is that a free independent EV assessment converts uncertain prospects into accepted claimants. Target conversion: ≥40% of submitted disputes proceed to Calmly offer acceptance. Pilot must measure and report this before at-scale content investment is committed.
Next step — R5

Proceed to R5 — Circumvent the Use Block (Adoption). The key question: what stops consumers who have decided to try from completing claim submission? Evidence assembly anxiety is the working hypothesis.

Note: R7 will recalculate blended CAC when gateway partner model is designed. The R4 CAC figure (£38) is a temporary ceiling — R7 is expected to reduce it.
